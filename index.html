When is the deadline for application?

You can apply until November 12th, 2022.

What does the program provide?

Support from a local or an international mentor, opportunity to network and form working groups with other participants of the program, environment where to grow, advance and broaden your skills and knowledge.

What is the selection criteria?

We evaluate your motivation and goals. The assessment will be carried out by a special working group together with mentors.

What's the timeline?

Program starts on 6th January and ends on 30th of June, 2023.

Whhat competences can I train in TechMentor program?

We have mentors representing 12 tracks: frontend development, backend development, UX/UI, Quality Assurance, engineering, IT support/DevOps, project management, product management, cyber security, data analytics/data science, business analytics, startups, "Step Up Your Game".

What is "Step Up Your Game" mentorship?

It's for ladies with some experience that want to "step up their game" in the IT field: boost their skillset, while knowing the basics already: likely finished our previous tracks, have done some work in the IT field or professionals that want to move to the IT field from a non-IT related position.
